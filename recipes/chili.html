Vegetarian Chili

Description

10 servings

A home chef's vegetarian take on Bobby Flay's Red Beef Chili. This spicy chili contains no animal products.

Ingredients

  • 2tbs. olive oil
  • 2tbs. ground cumin
  • 12oz. dark beer
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 2 Poblanos
  • 2 Anaheims
  • 2 Jalapenos
  • 2 Cubanos
  • 2 Banana peppers
  • 2-4 other peppers for variety
  • 1 chipotle in Adobe sauce
  • 2tbs. medium chili powder
  • 4tbs. hot chili powder
  • 7 tomatillos
  • 16oz. diced tomtoes
  • 3 15oz. cans of black beans
  • 2 15oz. cans of red kidney beans
  • 4-5 cups of water
  • 2tbs. unsweetened chocolate, finely chopped

Directions

  1. Roughly chop onion, peppers, garlic and tomatillos.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil on medium heat in a large pot.
  3. When oil is heated, add onions, peppers, and garlic. Stir every 15 minutes. Cook until soft for 45-60 minutes.
  4. Add chili powder, cumin, and tomatillos. Stir constantly for about 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add beer and Chipotle pepper in adobe sauce. Cook for about 20 mintes.
  6. Add tomatoes and water. Cook for 30 minutes.
  7. Puree the mixture to desired consistancy.
  8. Add beans and cook for 60 minutes.
  9. Mix in unsweetened chocolate and cook 5 more minutes.
